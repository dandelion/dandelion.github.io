Standalone mode

Since the v0.10.0, Dandelion-Datatables uses Dandelion Core to handle assets of your application. For some reasons, you may wish to disable the dependency mechanism and manage all client-side dependencies by yourself. Let's see how.

Actually, it's as simple as adding the property in your configuration file. 

main.standalone=true

As soon as your declare the above property, Dandelion-Datatables will use the exclusion mechanism of Dandelion Core for all the assets contained in the vendor bundles but datatables.

In this way, you can still use Dandelion-Datatables with your legacy assets.

<h3>Th attributes</h3>
<hr />

<h4>Description</h4>
<br />

The <code>&lt;th&gt;</code> attributes allow to configure the columns.

<br />
<h4>Usage</h4>

    <table dt:table="true" ...>
        <thead ...>
            <tr>
                <th>...</th>
                <th>...</th>
                <th dt:filterable="true">...</th>
                <th>...</th>
                <th>...</th>
            </tr>
        </thead>
        ...
    </table>

<br />

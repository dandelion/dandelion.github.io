Overview

Dandelion is a Java web framework aimed at applying - as easily as possible - best pratices of web developpement in JVM-based environments.

In this regard, Dandelion invites you to organize your web assets into bundles. Once all bundles prepared, Dandelion will help you to make your web application more responsive:

  • by injecting in an optimal way the <script> and <link> tags: usually stylesheets at the top and scripts at the bottom (configurable)
  • by performing typical optimizations such assets minification, assets merging or GZIP compression (in beta), whether your assets comes from the webapp, a JAR, a WebJar or a CDN

Dandelion is mainly based on a servlet filter which automatically injects assets into your HTML pages and is therefore considered as a runtime solution. But rather than making a long speech, take a look at the general overview below.

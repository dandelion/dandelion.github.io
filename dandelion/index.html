Overview

Dandelion is a Java web framework aimed at applying - as easily as possible - best pratices of web developpement in JVM-based environments.

In this regard, Dandelion will help you to:

  • organize your web assets in bundles
  • making your web application more responsive:
    • by injecting in an optimal way the <script> and <link> tags: usually stylesheets at the top and scripts at the bottom
    • by performing typical optimizations such assets minification, assets merging or GZIP compression (in beta), whether your assets comes from the webapp, a JAR, a WebJar or a CDN

Dandelion is mainly based on a servlet filter which automatically injects assets into your HTML pages. As such it is considered as a runtime solution. But rather than making a long speech, take a look at the general overview below.
